Why?

The SARS-CoV-2 virus has been spreading around the world, and we’re all trying to practice social distancing as best we can. Many folks are lonely, itching for an adrenaline rush, or just in need of a pick-me-up. Our Family may be spread wide, but we can all come together and thank our creator for fast cars.

We’ll start watching the films at the same time and chat in Discord along the way. There’s a lot of uncertainty out there, so we’re living our lives a quarter-mile at a time.
